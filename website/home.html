About Us

eSTEAM’d Bytes is on a mission to empower the next generation of leaders from underprivileged neighborhoods, grades 4 through 12, by providing them with access to cutting-edge STEAM education. We leverage accredited curriculums to equip our students with not only essential skills but also industry-recognized certifications, opening doors to limitless STEAM-related opportunities. As a black and women-owned business we are dedicated to bridging the diversity gap in STEAM fields and nurturing young minds to become innovators and changemakers. Our founders, experienced software engineers, bring their passion for STEAM and teaching to every classroom, ensuring that every student achieves their full potential and a brighter future.

Founders

Jasmine Moore (Jazzy Engineer)

With a background in software engineering and a graduate of the Resilient Coders Bootcamp, Jasmine Moore has dedicated her career to not only mastering the art of coding but also giving back to her community. She currently serves as a mentor in Resilient Coders' programs, guiding new cohorts, facilitating hackathons, and curating wellness workshops. Her journey from student to mentor showcases her commitment to empowering individuals through technology.

Theodore Williams

Theodore Williams is a career software engineer whose journey took him through Tech Elevator for training. He now contributes his expertise as a software engineer at Highmark, where he plays a pivotal role in the development of their dental applications, utilized by millions of customers. His experience exemplifies the potential that coding education can unlock, leading to impactful careers in the tech industry.
