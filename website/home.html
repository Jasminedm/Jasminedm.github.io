eSTEAM'd Bytes

eSTEAM’d Bytes is a dynamic and innovative education institution dedicated to providing high-quality STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) education to students in grades 4 through 12, with a particular focus on underprivileged neighborhoods. Founded by experienced software engineers, Jasmine Moore and Theodore Williams, our mission is to bridge the educational and opportunity gap in STEAM fields for underserved communities while promoting diversity and inclusion.

About Us

eSTEAM’d Bytes is on a mission to empower the next generation of leaders from underprivileged neighborhoods, grades 4 through 12, by providing them with access to cutting-edge STEAM education. We leverage accredited curriculums to equip our students with not only essential skills but also industry-recognized certifications, opening doors to limitless STEAM-related opportunities. As a black and women-owned business we are dedicated to bridging the diversity gap in STEAM fields and nurturing young minds to become innovators and changemakers. Our founders, experienced software engineers, bring their passion for STEAM and teaching to every classroom, ensuring that every student achieves their full potential and a brighter future.
